Our Journey

2023

The Beginning

SkyCast started as a university project with a simple goal: create a weather app that's both powerful and beautiful. Our team of passionate developers wanted to solve the complexity of weather data visualization.

2024

Launch & Growth

We launched our public beta and gained our first 1,000 users. Implemented real-time updates, expanded to 500+ cities, and introduced intelligent forecasting algorithms.

Future

AI & Innovation

Working on AI-powered predictions, personalized weather insights, and mobile applications. Our goal is to become Europe's most accurate and intuitive weather platform.
